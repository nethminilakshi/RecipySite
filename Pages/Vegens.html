Vegens

Vegan food recipes focus on plant-based ingredients that exclude all animal products, including meat, dairy, eggs, and honey, while still offering delicious and nutritious meal options. These recipes celebrate the flavors and textures of vegetables, fruits, grains, legumes, nuts, and seeds, making them suitable for a variety of tastes and dietary needs. Vegan food recipes not only promote health and environmental sustainability but also provide an opportunity to explore creative cooking techniques and diverse ingredients that bring vibrant flavors to the table.

Vegan banana pancakes

Make these foolproof vegan banana pancakes. A guaranteed crowd pleaser with a fluffy, light texture, you'd never know they’re dairy and egg-free

Ingredients:

  • 1 large ripe banana (around 150g)
  • 2 tbsp golden caster sugar
  • ¼ tsp fine salt
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil, plus extra for cooking
  • 120g self-raising flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 150ml oat, almond milk or soya milk
  • syrup, sliced banana and berries, to serve (optional)

How to make Vegan banana pancakes:

  1. Mash the banana in a mixing bowl. Stir in the sugar, salt and oil. Add
    the flour and baking powder and mix thoroughly. Make a well in the centre and gradually whisk in the milk. The batter should be a thick, droppable consistency.
  2. Heat a little oil in a frying pan over a medium heat. Add 2 tbsp of the batter to make American-style pancakes. You will be able to make about 4-5 at time.Fry on each side for 2-3 mins until golden. Serve with syrup, sliced banana and berries, if you like.
