Quick & Easy Foods

Quick and easy dishes are the perfect solution for busy lifestyles, offering delicious and satisfying meals without requiring hours of preparation or cooking time. One popular option is a stir-fry, where a mix of fresh vegetables—like bell peppers, broccoli, and snap peas—can be quickly sautéed with your choice of protein, such as chicken, shrimp, or tofu, and tossed in a flavorful sauce made from soy sauce, garlic, and ginger. Another great choice is a simple pasta dish; for instance, you can cook whole wheat spaghetti and toss it with olive oil, garlic, cherry tomatoes, and spinach for a light yet fulfilling meal ready in under 20 minutes.

Cheese Omelette

Rustle up a simple omelette for a quick and easy snack any time of day. Use mature cheddar for maximum cheesy flavour

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 500g self-raising flour
  • ½ tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 15g mature cheddar, finely grated

How to make Cheese Omelette:

  • Crack the eggs into a jug and whisk well with a fork. Season with a pinch of salt.
  • Heat the oil and butter in a medium non-stick frying pan over a medium-low heat. Once the butter has started to foam, pour in the eggs and tilt to cover the base of the pan. Using a spatula, gently draw in the eggs from four points so there are folds in the centre. Do this once or twice, then leave the eggs to cook gently for 2-3 mins, until there's a little raw egg still in the middle. Sprinkle over the cheese and, using your spatula, gently fold the omelette in half. Switch off the heat and let the residual heat from the pan melt the cheese for 1 min. Slide onto your plate and sprinkle over some black pepper to serve.
